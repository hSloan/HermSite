Who is HERM?

HERM is a helpful creature who does what ever he can for you from within a shell. His magic shell was passed down to him by Lord Linus Torvalds after making a vow to create helpful and logical ways to help humans excel in everyday life and business.

HERM, as an entity, is a software engineering company dedicated to providing technological services for small, private, and independently owned businesses. Some of the services provided include web development, software development, application development, database management, and server maintenance.
